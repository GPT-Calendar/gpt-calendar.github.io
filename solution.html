Our Solution

One AI Assistant That Actually Understands You

Smart Calendar combines natural language AI, location awareness, and financial intelligence into a single voice-first interface.

Key Features

Voice-First

Speak Naturally, Get Things Done

Say "Kiro, remind me to call John tomorrow at 3 PM" — even with your phone in your pocket. Our custom wake word detection works offline.

  • Custom "Kiro" wake word (works offline)
  • Natural language understanding via LLM
  • Supports Ollama (local) or OpenAI
  • Text-to-speech responses
Location-Aware

Reminders That Know Where You Are

Geofencing-powered alerts trigger when you arrive or leave locations. "Remind me to buy milk when I reach any grocery store."

  • Named places (home, work, gym)
  • Generic categories (any store, pharmacy)
  • Custom radius and time constraints
  • Battery-optimized geofencing
Smart Finance

Automatic Expense Tracking

AI parses bank SMS messages to automatically detect and categorize transactions. Or just say "I spent 50 on lunch" for manual entries.

  • Auto-parse bank SMS notifications
  • Budget tracking with 80% warnings
  • Spending insights and analytics
  • Category-based reporting
